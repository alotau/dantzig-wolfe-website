---
term: master problem
shortDef: >
  The top-level linear programme in Dantzig-Wolfe Decomposition that selects a
  convex combination of extreme points from sub-problem feasible regions while
  satisfying the coupling constraints.
relatedTerms:
  - restricted master problem
  - dual variables
  - coupling constraints
  - column generation
---

The **master problem** (MP) is the reformulated LP obtained by replacing each
sub-problem's variable block with a convex combination of its extreme points
$\{v_k^j\}$:

$$
\min_{(\lambda_k^j)} \sum_{k=1}^{K} \sum_j (c_k^{\top} v_k^j)\, \lambda_k^j
\quad \text{s.t.} \quad
A_0 \sum_{k,j} v_k^j \lambda_k^j \leq b_0, \;
\sum_j \lambda_k^j = 1 \; \forall k, \;
\lambda_k^j \geq 0.
$$

In practice only a small subset of extreme points is kept at any time; this is
the **restricted master problem** (RMP).
