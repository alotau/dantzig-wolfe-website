---
term: coupling constraints
shortDef: >
  The rows of a block-angular LP that link variables from different sub-problem
  blocks; they prevent each block from being solved in complete isolation.
relatedTerms:
  - block-angular LP
  - master problem
  - dual variables
---

**Coupling constraints** (also called _linking constraints_) are the rows of the
block-angular constraint matrix that involve variables from more than one block:

$$
A_0 x \leq b_0, \quad x = (x_1, \ldots, x_K)
$$

In Dantzig-Wolfe Decomposition the coupling constraints are handled by the
**master problem**. Their associated dual variables $\pi$ serve as price signals
passed to the sub-problems during column generation.
